Schedule Ahead


Schedule ahead - As you get homework you should write it down along with when it’s due or needs to be done by.
On an afternoon when you do your homework you should do the work that is due first before the rest. Ask a teacher if you are unsure and don't be shy to.

Write a checklist
this doesn’t just help with organization but to feel accomplished. As you complete your homework or assignments you can feel just that bit better once you complete something by checking it off

Get a white board
write down when your assignments are due and what you need to do to complete them to stay organized and keep on top of your work. You can also write time a schedule of when you can do what work of a night

Ask questions
always ensure your asking questions if you are ever confused or stuck with your assignments or classwork. You will not learn unless you understand the work that is given to you

Revise
before each test or after a class and you learn something new, revise over it so you can remember it. If it is a difficult topic or test, spend that extra bit longer so you feel more confident going into the test knowing you have had time to revise over what you have learnt in class. Use the helpful sources that your teachers put on SEQTA or save the worksheets and PowerPoints that are for each lesson so it is easier to find when you need them.
