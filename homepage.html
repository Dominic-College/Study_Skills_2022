Schedule Ahead

Schedule ahead - As you get homework you should write it down along with when it’s due or needs to be done by.
On an afternoon when you do your homework you should do the work that is due first before the rest. Ask a teacher if you are unsure and don't be shy to ask. Here is a template for a timetable
Here

Write a checklist

This doesn’t just help with your organization but to feel accomplished. As you complete your homework or assignments you can feel just that bit better once you complete something by checking it off.

Get a white board


Write down when your assignments are due and what you need to do to complete them to stay organized and keep on top of your work. You can also write time a schedule of when you can do what works in a night.

Ask questions


Always ensure your asking questions if you are ever confused or stuck with your assignments or classwork. You will not learn unless you understand the work that is given to you.

Revise


Before each test or after a class and you learn something new, revise over it so you can remember it. If it is a difficult topic or test, spend that extra bit longer so you feel more confident going into the test knowing you have had time to revise over what you have learnt in class. Use the helpful sources that your teachers put on SEQTA or save the worksheets and PowerPoints that are for each lesson so it is easier to find when you need them. You can use Quizlet to help you with flash cards or make your own.
